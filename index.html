ABOUT US

Utah Airlines was founded in 1983 By Kyle Howard and Jim Stuart. They were best friends in highschool and they decided they wanted to make air travel cheaper, and more accessible to people. they founded Utah Airlines to achieve this goal for the people in utah. They plan to fly to more places around the world in the near future, giving people the low price, but high quality they all deserve. Our airlines operates at airports all over the United States of America including the Airport you know and love, the Salt Lake City airport
